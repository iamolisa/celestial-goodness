Sacred Boundaries

Ethics & Disclaimer

Transparency, integrity, and sacred boundaries are at the heart of this practice. Please read carefully before booking.

1. The Nature of This Work

Celestial Goodness Astrology and Tarot offers astrology readings, tarot and oracle card readings, and related spiritual guidance sessions. These services are provided for entertainment, educational, and personal reflection purposes only. Astrology and tarot are ancient symbolic systems used as tools for self-reflection and personal insight. They are not sciences, and no reading constitutes a guarantee of any particular outcome. The information offered in sessions is intended to support your own thinking and decision-making, not to replace it.

2. Not a Substitute for Professional Advice

Sessions with Heather are not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, legal, financial, or therapeutic advice. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, serious illness, legal difficulty, or financial emergency, please seek appropriate professional support. Heather does not diagnose, prescribe, treat, or cure any condition.

3. Free Will & Personal Responsibility

Heather does not claim to predict the future or dictate fate. Astrology and tarot illuminate patterns, cycles, and possibilities. You retain full free will and personal responsibility for every decision you make. The guidance offered in sessions is intended to help you access your own inner wisdom. The answers are coming from within you. You are the author of your life.

4. Confidentiality

Everything shared within a session is held in the strictest confidence. Heather considers each session a sacred and private conversation. Your personal information, birth details, and the content of your reading will never be shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

5. Tarot Reading Frequency Policy

Out of care for the integrity of the work and your wellbeing, Heather will not conduct multiple tarot readings for the same client within the same calendar quarter. This boundary exists to encourage integration of insights received, not dependence on repeated readings. Astrology-based sessions are not subject to this restriction.

6. Booking, Payment & Cancellation

  • ✦Full payment is required at the time of booking.
  • ✦24-hour advance notice is required to reschedule.
  • ✦One reschedule permitted per booking.
  • ✦Missed appointments are non-refundable.
  • ✦All sessions conducted via Zoom (primary). Phone available upon request.

7. Accuracy of Birth Data

For sessions involving natal chart analysis, reading accuracy depends on the accuracy of your date, exact time, and location of birth. If your exact birth time is unknown, Heather will cast an Aries Rising chart. Please provide the most accurate information available.

8. Age Requirement

Services are intended for clients 18 years of age or older. By booking a session, you confirm you meet this requirement, or that you are a parent or legal guardian booking on behalf of a minor with full knowledge and consent.

9. Emotional Wellbeing

Heather approaches every session with compassion and profound respect for the sacred nature of what is shared. Sessions may occasionally bring up deep emotions or challenging themes. If you are in a vulnerable emotional state or actively in crisis, please reach out to a qualified mental health professional before booking.

10. Right to Decline Service

Celestial Goodness reserves the right to decline or discontinue service to any individual at any time, at its sole discretion, particularly in cases of disrespectful conduct or requests outside the ethical scope of this practice.

By booking a session, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to these terms.

These terms exist not to limit the magic of our work together, but to protect it. Sacred space requires sacred boundaries. Thank you for honoring them.

— Heather Wiggins, Celestial Goodness Astrology & Tarot
