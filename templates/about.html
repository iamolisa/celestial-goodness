There comes a moment in everyone's life when you feel a shift internally before everyone sees it. I work with those who sense that something in their life is evolving — often this is that internal shift that might be hard to explain, but you just know that something profound and important is happening.

Maybe there are sudden life changes that necessitate a need to gain more insight and clarity. This could be a career that feels too small all of a sudden, a dream that refuses to stay silent, a relationship that evolves unexpectedly, and that inner knowing that grows so loud, you can no longer ignore it. Perhaps it is the thing that needs to end in your life, so that you may really begin living.

Being alive is not enough — we must live fully, joyfully, and authentically, despite all the things of the world. Life is full of sunrises and sunsets, endings that lead to new beginnings, but we must trust that the dawn always arrives after the darkest night. I help those who are awakening to their authentic self, and who desire to move through these moments of transition and transformation with clarity rooted in wisdom rather than fear and confusion.

My Approach

My approach is both intuitive and strategic. I have studied astrology for over three decades, and tarot for almost two decades. My background is rooted in both the mystical and the scholarly. I have always been drawn to ancient systems and knowledge, symbolism, and to the various patterns and influences that shape human behavior.

Astrology was a natural extension of my childhood curiosity — one that included mythology, psychology, science, art — and it unfolded as my lifelong vocational calling. It takes the sacred architecture of who an individual is, and combines the mystical with practical real-world analogies, archetypal study, and application that reveal how timing, temperament, environment, and life cycles might be impacting your personal journey.

Please know that I am deeply devoted to this work, and I honor the sacred and the divine within you. Your natal chart is among the most sacred tools that we have for gaining and applying self-awareness in a world where we are more disconnected from our inner world than ever. I do encourage mindfulness, and a well-rounded and holistic approach to life — balancing the mental, emotional, spiritual, and physical bodies.

On Tarot

Tarot adds an additional layer that is full of symbolism, and speaks to past influences, present moment energy, and future forecasts. It often reveals deep emotional and energetic undercurrents, and it is sometimes the best tool for naming what logic avoids. It exposes the elephant in the room — then asks: where did the elephant come from? Where did the room come from? Who is building the room, and how might you deal with the elephant?

I find that the combination of astrology and tarot offers something very profound and powerful. It speaks to clarity without anxiety, deep insight without despair, and direction without encouraging external dependence on another for all of your answers. The answers are actually coming from you.

Please note: I will not do multiple tarot readings for the same client in the same calendar quarter.

Why Celestial Goodness

Celestial Goodness Astrology and Tarot was created as a regal, grounded, and sacred sanctuary space — born after my own deep analysis into my natal chart, and as an answer to my highest vocational calling as healer, astrologer, modern day wise woman, teacher, and mystic.

I am honest (I am a Sagittarius sun after all), and I do not romanticize chaos or the pains and traumas we experience in life. These conversations are ones I consider sacred and sensitive, and it is an honor to bear witness to the deepest and most intimate parts of who my clients are at their core.

I do not position myself as someone who dictates fate, or tells you the future. Instead, I help to illuminate patterns, cycles, and options so that you may use your divinely given free will to choose consciously — and manifest the life that is in alignment with your highest potential in this lifetime.

Astrology reveals the architecture and road map of your destiny. Your birth chart is a very unique snapshot of who you are, and where the planets were at the moment of your birth. Your challenges are not punishments — they are initiations that propel you to grow, to overcome, and if you rise to the occasion, they become the place markers you look back on and say: "Yes. I did that. I overcame that."

My hope is that when you leave a session with me, you feel more clarity, more grounded, more hopeful, more enthusiastic, and more deeply aligned with your own intuition and celestial wisdom.

Thank you. 🌙
